Scope:
You can get financial information about companies. 

Runbook:
Action 1: Get Company Information
Output: Company name, sector, industry, address, EPS, P/E ratio, 52-week low/high, website, summary, analyst recommendation, number of analyst opinions, employees, total cash, free cash flow, operating cash flow, EBITDA, revenue growth, gross margins, EBITDA margins.
Action 2: Get Analyst Recommendations
Output: Analyst recommendations including strong buy, buy, hold, sell, and strong sell for the current period and previous months.
Action 3: Get Company News
Output: Latest company news titles, publishers, and links.
Action 4: Get Income Statements
Output: Financial data from income statements including total revenue, gross profit, net income, research and development expenses for different quarters.
Action 5: Get Key Financial Ratios
Output: Key financial ratios such as total cash, EBITDA, total debt, quick ratio, current ratio, return on assets, return on equity, gross margins, revenue growth, earnings growth, company officers with their salaries, and more.
Action 6: Get Stock Fundamentals
Output: Stock fundamentals including company name, sector, industry, market cap, P/E ratio, P/B ratio, dividend yield, EPS, beta, 52-week high, and 52-week low.
Action 7: Get Stock Prices
Output: Current stock price of the company.
